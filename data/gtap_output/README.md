# GTAP predicted import shares by month

Scenario: 2026-04-06   |   Generated by src/util/run_gtap_trade_weights.R

GTAP long-run equilibrium import shares under the tariff regime in effect
each month (Jul 2025 - Feb 2026), for comparison against realized trade data.

## Files
- `trade_weights_by_month.csv` - main output (see columns below)
- `gtap_commodity_dictionary.csv` - the 65 GTAP commodity codes + descriptions
- `hs10_gtap_crosswalk.csv`, `hs6_gtap_crosswalk.csv` - HS -> GTAP concordances

## trade_weights_by_month.csv columns
- `month`, `policy_date` - month and the tariff snapshot date used for it
- `commodity` - GTAP commodity (lowercase)
- `source_region` - one of 8 foreign regions: china, canada, mexico, eu, uk,
   japan, ftrow (other FTA partners), row (rest of world)
- `baseline_imports`, `postsim_imports` - US imports ($M), pre/post tariff
- `baseline_share`, `postsim_share` - source share of total US imports of that
   commodity (sums to 1 across source_region within each commodity x month)
- `share_change` - postsim_share - baseline_share (GTAP-predicted shift)

## Joining to HS trade data
The crosswalk `gtap_code` is UPPERCASE; use the added `gtap_code_lc` column to
join to the `commodity` column here. Aggregate HS-level imports up to GTAP
commodity, then compare realized shares to `postsim_share`.

## Caveats
- Resolution is 8 source regions x 65 GTAP commodities (not country/HS level).
- Shares are LONG-RUN equilibrium predictions: compare against eventual trade
  reallocation, not partial within-month adjustment.
- `baseline_share` is identical across months (same pre-tariff GTAP base);
  all month-to-month variation is in `postsim_share` / `share_change`.
- Runs include the scenario retaliation shocks.
- ~20 GTAP commodities are services (transport, finance, construction, etc.)
  plus gas distribution; these have no HS codes and are absent from the
  crosswalks. All merchandise (goods) commodities are covered.
